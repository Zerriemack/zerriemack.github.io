About

This site is my personal hub for projects, course work, and curated sources. I built it to keep everything organized in one place so instructors, recruiters, and visitors can quickly find what I am working on without digging through multiple repositories.

How I use this hub

The Projects section is the main entry point. It includes links to my active builds and portfolio work, including Milly Maker Tracker, which is a website project focused on organizing DraftKings Milly Maker contest winners and lineup data for research and analysis.

I keep Sources and Notes separate so it is easy to find curated references and coursework material without mixing them into my project links.
